Nodito Labs is a validator group running on the Celo network founded by early employees of cLabs. We seek to:

  • Promote decentralization and credibility of the network
  • Grow the global Celo community
  • Offer the opportunity for qualified validators to build their brand
  • Support causes we believe in

Each validator in our group has committed to donating 10% of their rewards to a non-profit
